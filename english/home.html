Welcome to Osheaga 2024

Dive into the heart of Montreal's vibrant music scene at Osheaga, one of Canada's premier music and arts festivals! Held every summer at Parc Jean-Drapeau, this three-day event brings together a stellar lineup of international headliners, rising stars, and eclectic genres, from indie and pop to hip-hop and electronic.
Beyond the music, Osheaga offers an unforgettable experience with stunning outdoor stages, delicious food trucks, captivating art installations, and the electric energy of festivalgoers from around the globe.
Don't miss out! Book your tickets now and make memories that will last a lifetime.
